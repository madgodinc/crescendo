The flywheel · Crescendo

Every time the orchestra recovers from a failure, it writes the fix to memory. Next time that class of problem appears, it recalls the fix instead of rediscovering it, so it gets cheaper and more accurate the longer it runs. The numbers below are operational stats over the 3 recorded runs on this instance, not a benchmark — small N, our own briefs, our own checks. They show the mechanism working, not a score.
